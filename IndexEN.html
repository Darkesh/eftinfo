ABOUT OUR HOTEL

Hotelima is located in the center of Almada and offers a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere, with a team of staff always available to help during your stay.

All dormitories and rooms have electronic locks, private light, and include access to bathhouse (Male and Female - separated).

Hotelima offers free Wi-Fi throughout the Hostel, kitchen, business center with printer and also a TV room and game consoles.

We have a 24 hour reception.
